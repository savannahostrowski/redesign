Rhododendron

Dec. 2015 - present

This is a collaborative effort between me and my partner, William McDonald. Rhododendron is a web app which will allow users to plot their data on a daily basis and provide them with a chart to visualize their information. Tech used includes: Python/Bottle, mongodb, HTML5 and CSS3.

Sustainable Development in Waterloo Region Web App

July 2015 - Aug. 2015

This was a small web app I built as part of the culminating project for the GEOG 481 - Geographic Information Systems Project course at the University of Waterloo. My team and I conducted a comprehensive study on how students have changed the Waterloo region in terms of sustainable development. This study used census data to quantify impact from 1996 to 2011. Tech used: Javascript/Leaflet.js, HTML5 and CSS3.
